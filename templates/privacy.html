We use cookies to maintain your login session and remember your preferences. A cookie is a small text file placed on your device by our web server. You may decline cookies through your browser settings, though this may prevent you from logging in or using certain features.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information and uploaded data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

Data Retention

We retain your account information and uploaded data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your data at any time from within the application. Upon account deletion, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems.
